About

This project began as a class assignment for a course on Globalization and Human Rights. The goal: raising money and awarenss for Syrian refugees. The team began thinking about how we could do this. We came up with numerous fundraising ideas: asking resteraunts in the area to donate a portion of their profits to the cause, asking for individual donations by emailing classmates, and asking friends and family. But we realized the awareness factor is just as important. Do people really know what these refugees experience? We decided to set up tables in our school's student center that will give students the opportunity to try and experience what refugees do. Through that and this website, we feel we're not only asking people for money, but asking them to learn and understand. It's through education and understanding that we can generate meaningful change.
